Content Requirements

Guidelines for reviewing and approving content

To ensure your content meets quality standards and can be published quickly, please review the following requirements and guidelines.

Review Timeline

Content must be reviewed within 48 hours of submission
Urgent content (marked red) requires review within 24 hours
After approval, content will be published within 1-2 business days

Content Types

Website Content

Blog posts, service pages, landing pages, and website copy updates

Google Business Profile

Business updates, posts, offers, and event announcements

Social Posts

Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn posts with captions and hashtags

AI Creative

AI-generated graphics, banners, social images, and promotional visuals

Approval Guidelines

Verify all facts, dates, and contact information are accurate
Check that pricing and promotional details are correct
Ensure brand voice and tone are consistent
Review for spelling and grammatical errors
Confirm all links and calls-to-action are appropriate

Rejection Process

1 If content needs changes, click "Reject" and provide specific feedback
2 Our team will revise and resubmit within 24-48 hours
3 You'll receive a notification when revised content is ready for review

Need Help?

If you have questions about any content or need to discuss changes before approval, use the chat widget in the bottom right corner of your screen to message our team directly.
